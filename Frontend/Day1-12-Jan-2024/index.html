Big Cat => Lion

The lion, scientifically known as Panthera leo, stands as one of the most iconic and majestic creatures in the animal kingdom. Renowned as the "king of the jungle," lions inhabit grasslands, savannas, and open woodlands across Africa and a small population in the Gir Forest National Park in India.
Known for their distinctive manes, which vary in color and length among individuals, male lions exude strength and dominance, while females play an integral role in the pride's social structure as skilled hunters and caretakers. Lions are highly social animals, forming family groups called prides that consist of related females, their cubs, and a coalition of males. These powerful predators are known for their impressive roars that can be heard from miles away, serving both as a means of communication and a declaration of territory.Despite their regal appearance, lions face various conservation challenges, including habitat loss, human-wildlife conflict, and poaching .
